Privacy Policy

This statement pertains to HSC Advisory

At HSC Advisory, safeguarding your privacy is our utmost concern, and we are committed to preserving the confidentiality and security of the personal information you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy delineates how HSC Advisory collects, manages, and safeguards your personal data in relation to our digital platform and our comprehensive range of business consultancy services, including corporate restructuring, economic analysis, forensic investigations, litigation support, healthcare solutions, strategic communications, and technological services ("Professional Services").

Applicability of this Statement

This statement applies to personal data that HSC Advisory collects and/or utilizes as a data controller, as defined by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar legislation (e.g., Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados, the DIFC Data Protection Law of 2020, etc.). California residents should review the dedicated section addressing their specific rights. When delivering Professional Services, we occasionally handle personal data as a data processor (e.g., hosting client information on our e-discovery platforms), meaning we process it solely under our clients' directives. If our use of your personal data is not covered by this statement, you may need to contact the client (and data controller) responsible for your information. For the purposes of this statement, "Personal Data" refers to information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual or can reasonably be associated or linked with a specific person or household.

Digital Tracking Policy

HSC Advisory is dedicated to protecting the privacy of your personal data and maintaining transparency regarding the technologies it employs. This Digital Tracking Policy elucidates how and why HSC Advisory utilizes cookies and similar technologies to enhance and optimize your user experience when interacting with our digital platforms.

Our digital platform employs cookies and comparable tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze platform traffic, and tailor content. You can manage your preferences for these technologies through your browser configuration.

Data Protection Measures

HSC Advisory implements appropriate technical safeguards, security protocols, and procedures to protect personal data from loss, misuse, unauthorized alteration, or destruction. Our measures include confidentiality requirements for personnel and service providers, access restrictions based on necessity, and appropriate training for our staff. We maintain comprehensive data incident response protocols, and our IT security program undergoes periodic audits.

Your Data Rights

You are entitled to the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: The right to obtain your personal data.
  • Rectification: The right to correct inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Erasure: The right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Objection: The right to oppose the processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, widely-used, and machine-readable format.

Data Retention Policy

HSC Advisory retains your personal data for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, for any compatible purposes subsequently identified, or for any new purposes you subsequently consent to. Additionally, retention may be required for legal, regulatory, and internal policy compliance. Generally, this period aligns with the duration of your relationship or contract with us, plus an additional period reflecting the time during which legal claims might be brought post-termination. Certain records, such as call recordings, tax documents, and compliance-related information, may need to be retained longer. Retention periods may also be adjusted based on legal requirements or data protection impact assessments.

Minors' Data Protection

HSC Advisory's digital platforms are not designed for or directed at individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information about persons under 16, except as part of providing professional services under specific engagements.

Amendments to this Statement

By utilizing the HSC Advisory digital platforms, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy and elsewhere on our digital platforms. HSC Advisory reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this digital platform.

Contact Information

If you have any inquiries or feedback regarding our Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at info@hscconsult.co.za.
